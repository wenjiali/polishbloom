Become a Member

An invitation to intentional community and growth.

Why is Polish + Bloom invite-only?

At Polish + Bloom, we value alignment, trust, and intentional community. That's why membership is by invitation only—referred by a current member or a trusted partner. This ensures our circle remains a safe, inspiring space for meaningful growth.

Once referred, you'll complete a brief vetting process so we can understand your goals and how we can best support your journey.

This isn't about exclusivity for appearance—it's about curating the right environment for transformation to truly take root.

Our Vetting Process

Simple, personal, and designed to honor your journey.

1

Intro Questionnaire

You'll complete a short form sharing your interests, goals, and what brought you to Polish + Bloom. This helps us understand how we can best support your growth—financially, emotionally, and professionally.

2

Discovery Conversation (15–20 mins)

We'll invite you to a casual video or voice chat with a member of our team. This is a chance for mutual alignment—to hear your story, answer your questions, and explore what you're looking for in this season of life.

3

Match & Welcome

If it's a great fit, you'll be matched with the right program, circle, or resource to begin your journey. You'll receive a warm welcome, access to our private member space, and introductions to other aligned members.

Our goal is not to judge, but to connect. We're here to ensure you land in a space that feels safe, empowering, and aligned with your personal values and vision for growth.
