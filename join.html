Everything you need to know about joining our community

Because real transformation happens in sacred space.

When women gather to heal their relationship with money, vulnerability is required. Fear, shame, and limiting beliefs around finances can only be addressed when you feel completely safe.

In our Bloom Circles, you'll find:

  • Zero judgment about your financial past or present
  • Complete confidentiality — what's shared stays sacred
  • Shared commitment to growth, not just advice-seeking
  • Mutual support through both struggles and celebrations

This level of trust can't exist with open enrollment. Every member is carefully matched to ensure:

  • Similar values around personal growth and money
  • Genuine commitment to showing up authentically
  • Respect for the sacred nature of this work

It's not about exclusivity—it's about creating the exact conditions where financial healing and empowerment can actually happen.

Simple, personal, and designed to honor your journey.

  • Intro Questionnaire: Complete a thoughtful form about your interests, goals, and what drew you here. This helps us understand how to best support your growth.
  • Discovery Chat: A casual 15-20 minute conversation with our team to share your story, ask questions, and explore mutual alignment.
  • Welcome & Match: If it's a great fit, you'll be matched with the perfect program or circle, plus get warm introductions to other aligned members.

Our goal isn't to judge—it's to connect you with a space that feels safe, empowering, and perfectly aligned with your values.

Through someone who knows us.

Ask any current member or trusted partner organization. Our members genuinely love sharing this community with friends who would benefit. Common referral sources include:

  • Wellness practitioners & coaches
  • Women's professional networks
  • Financial advisors & planners
  • Career development consultants

If you know someone in our community, simply mention your interest—they'll be happy to connect you with us.

You can still reach out directly.

While referrals are our primary path, we occasionally consider direct applications from aligned individuals. We also partner with organizations and practitioners who can refer suitable candidates.

For direct outreach, please share your story, what drew you here, and how you align with our values of trust, growth, and mutual support.

We read every application thoughtfully, even without a formal referral.
