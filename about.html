Everything you need to know about joining our community

Our community is built on trust and vulnerability. An invite-only model allows us to cultivate a safe space where every member is committed to confidentiality and mutual support. This isn't about exclusivity; it's about creating the most effective environment for personal and financial transformation.

Our process is simple, personal, and designed to ensure a perfect fit for everyone:

  1. Submit your application - Share your goals, challenges, and what draws you to our community
  2. Personal review - We carefully review your application within 2-3 business days
  3. Welcome call - If it's a good fit, we'll schedule a brief 15-minute call
  4. Join your circle - Get matched to your perfect Bloom Circle and create your account

Start your application here

Most referrals come from current members or our network of trusted partners, such as coaches and financial advisors. If you know someone in our community, we encourage you to ask them about us. They can guide you on the next steps.

Have a referral? Be sure to mention their name in your application form!

You're absolutely welcome to apply! While many members are referred, we thoughtfully review every direct application. If our mission resonates with you, please fill out our application form and tell us about your journey.

We believe the right people find us when they're ready, and we're excited to learn about what brought you here.

We offer both complimentary community memberships and paid premium programs. This ensures our core support is accessible, while also providing options for deeper, more personalized guidance. All financial commitments are discussed transparently during the matching process to ensure they feel aligned and sustainable for you.

The entire process typically takes 3-5 business days:

  • Application review: 2-3 business days
  • Welcome call scheduling: Usually within 1-2 days
  • Account creation: Same day as your welcome call

We'll keep you updated throughout the process, so you'll never wonder what's happening next.
